# Research Agenda

The Verittà Think Tank is interested in AI governance not as abstract commentary alone, but as operational practice.

## Research lines

### 1. Operational AI Governance
How can governance be embedded into the execution layer of agentic systems rather than added as after-the-fact policy language?

### 2. Fail-Closed Systems
How should AI systems behave when scope, evidence, identity, safety or accountability conditions are missing?

### 3. Human Sovereignty in Agentic Environments
What does meaningful human-in-the-loop actually require beyond interface-level approval buttons?

### 4. Debt Governance
How should technical, documental, cognitive and governance debt be identified, prioritized and made legible over time?

### 5. Source-of-Truth Architecture
How do complex ecosystems preserve canonical precedence without collapsing into documentation drift?

### 6. Reinstantiation and Continuity
How can new sessions, agents or operators recover sufficient context without pretending to possess perfect continuity?

### 7. Public Framework Translation
How can a private operational method be translated into a public framework without exposing sensitive internals or reducing it to marketing language?

## Working style
- research before rhetoric;
- architecture before branding;
- publishable claims before promotional inflation;
- clear boundaries between private canon and public layer.
