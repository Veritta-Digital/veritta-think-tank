# Public Method

## Purpose
This document explains how the public layer of the Verittà Think Tank is derived from the deeper private canon without pretending to reproduce it in full.

## Principle
The public layer is not invented from scratch.
It is forged from private operational reality through curation, redaction, abstraction and editorial discipline.

## Source layers
The deeper private canon may include:
- source-of-truth documents;
- contracts and seals;
- execution notes;
- diaries and transfer orders;
- architecture and governance records.

## Transformation pipeline
Public material should pass through these stages:

1. **Selection**  
   Identify whether the underlying material has real public value.

2. **Redaction**  
   Remove secrets, topology, client-identifiable data, operationally sensitive specifics and unnecessary backstage detail.

3. **Abstraction**  
   Translate the material from internal language into concepts that an external qualified reader can understand.

4. **Editorial stabilization**  
   Reduce drift, align terminology with the canon and preserve distinctions such as fact, inference and hypothesis when relevant.

5. **Human approval**  
   Public release remains a human decision.

## What this method avoids
- publishing raw diaries as if they were public essays;
- mistaking internal sovereignty for public authority;
- marketing inflation that outpaces operational truth;
- leaking sensitive internals under the label of transparency.

## Result
The public layer becomes trustworthy precisely because it is bounded, derived and governed.
