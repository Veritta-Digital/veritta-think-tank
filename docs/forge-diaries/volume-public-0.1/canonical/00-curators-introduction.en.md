# Forge Diaries - Public Volume 0.1
## Curator's Introduction

This public edition should not be mistaken for a raw archive of the forge.
It is a governed crossing from a private operational canon into a public editorial form.

The volume therefore preserves asymmetry on purpose.
It offers a readable sequence, not the whole backstage.
It favors threshold, architecture and legibility over exhaustive disclosure.

Volume Public 0.1 is organized around four movements:
- constitutional emergence;
- adversarial pressure;
- governance, memory and form;
- the threshold to public language.

Together they show how a serious system begins to differentiate itself, test itself, preserve its own reasoning and eventually speak in public without abandoning rigor.
