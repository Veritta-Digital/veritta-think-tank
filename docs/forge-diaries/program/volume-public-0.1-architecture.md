# Forge Diaries - Public Volume 0.1 Architecture

## Working title
**Diarios da Forja - Public Volume 0.1**  
**Foundation, Adversariality and the Threshold to Public Language**

## Editorial purpose
This volume should not function as a raw archive.
It should function as a governed public edition: a curated sequence that preserves the forge's conceptual originality while respecting the distinction between public intelligibility and private canonical depth.

## Editorial principle
The volume must not pretend completeness.
Its task is to establish a trustworthy first public form.

## Editorial apparatus
Each part of the volume should include:
- a short curator's introduction;
- the public-facing text or curated derivative;
- light contextual notes where necessary;
- visible redaction discipline when material was transformed rather than reproduced.

## Canonical assembly
The volume's canonical home is now:

- `docs/forge-diaries/volume-public-0.1/ASSEMBLY_MAP.md`

## What should remain outside Volume 0.1
- raw diary dumps;
- live secrets or operational detail;
- internal-only adversarial critique that is still too close to active architecture;
- material whose main value is evidentiary rather than editorial.

## Immediate next step
Use this architecture to guide the next drafting wave rather than to force premature closure.
Volume 0.1 should emerge by curated accretion, not by brute aggregation.
