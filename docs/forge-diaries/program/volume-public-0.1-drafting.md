# Forge Diaries - Public Volume 0.1 Drafting

## Purpose
This drafting note begins the transition from architectural outline to actual volume construction.

It does not close the volume.
It identifies the first curatorially stable drafting moves.

## Curatorial opening note - provisional
The public edition of the Forge Diaries should not be mistaken for a raw archive.
It is better understood as a governed crossing: a passage from a private operational canon into a public editorial form that preserves tension, originality and structure without exposing the full backstage of the forge.

The task of the volume is therefore double.
It must remain faithful to the deeper canon from which it derives, and it must become readable to those who did not inhabit that canon from the inside.

This is why the volume begins not with the loudest later texts, but with the earliest differentiated voices that explain what the forge is becoming: execution, order, containment and infrastructure.

## Part II - drafting guidance
**Part title:** The Adversarial Crucible

### Format decision
The first public-facing piece of Part II should enter as a **volume chapter**, not as a stand-alone essay and not as a casebook.

### Reason for the format decision
- it depends on cumulative tension rather than a single discrete event;
- it draws force from multiple sources that illuminate one another;
- its best public function is to deepen the volume's middle architecture rather than compete with the already promoted essays.

### Core nucleus selected for first drafting wave
1. `2026-03-15_c27-custo-do-correto-multidimensional.md`
2. `2026-03-16_noesis-liberdade-governanca-proposito.md`
3. `CHAMADO_A_GUARDIA_20260320.md`

### Why this nucleus
This trio is enough to establish the central thesis of the adversarial layer without prematurely overloading the section with every Red Team artifact.

- `c27` contributes silent error, layered correctness and adversarial epistemics.
- `NOESIS - liberdade` contributes the governance/freedom tension in explicit form.
- `CHAMADO_A_GUARDIA` contributes multi-agent review discipline and the image of the Red Team pressing against the gates.

### Status of additional material
- `CHAT-002_tema-livre-pos-megacontrato.md` remains valuable as supporting substrate.
- Athena's MCP adversarial material remains too close to active architecture for early inclusion.
