# Portuguese Translation Position

## Recommendation
Prepare Portuguese not as an afterthought, but as a first-class publication lane.

## Why
The immediate likely audience for Verittà's thought leadership on LinkedIn is Brazilian and Portuguese-speaking.
That makes Portuguese strategically important for first-wave reach, clarity and institutional positioning.

## Translation strategy
### Short term
Translate outward-facing assets first:
- launch post;
- curator's introduction;
- release note;
- abstract/summary of the volume.

### Medium term
Translate the two promoted essays:
- Why Serious AI Governance Must Capture Decision Rationale
- Governance Does Not Eliminate Freedom

### Longer term
Decide whether the whole volume should exist in:
- bilingual parallel form;
- Portuguese-first with English derivatives;
- or English-first with Portuguese public extracts.

## Recommended immediate choice
Portuguese-first for LinkedIn outward publication.
English remains useful for international positioning and later derivative publication.
