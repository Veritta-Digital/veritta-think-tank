# LinkedIn Newsletter Concept — EN

## Working newsletter title
**Verittà — Agentic AI Governance Notes**

## Description
A recurring LinkedIn newsletter on agentic AI governance, continuity, evidence, fail-closed systems, and the public architecture of the Forge Diaries and V-COF.

## Why a newsletter makes sense
The Forge Diaries line and the V-COF line are not one-post topics.
They benefit from recurring publication with continuity across editions.

## Suggested first editions
1. Curator's Introduction to *Forge Diaries — Public Volume 0.1*
2. Why serious AI governance must capture decision rationale
3. Governance does not eliminate freedom
4. What operational AI governance looks like

## Cadence suggestion
Monthly or twice-monthly.

## Language strategy
English can function as:
- a second language lane;
- or the international-facing newsletter after Portuguese traction is established.
