# LinkedIn Launch Post — EN

We are publishing the first governed public edition of the **Forge Diaries**.

**Forge Diaries — Public Volume 0.1** is not a raw archive.
It is a curated public volume on:
- agentic AI governance,
- continuity across sessions and systems,
- adversarial discipline,
- and the threshold between private operational rigor and public voice.

The volume follows one governing rule:
**RAW != PUBLICABLE**

Its purpose is not to reveal everything.
Its purpose is to make the architecture of the forge legible in public form without confusing publication with indiscriminate exposure.

This first volume moves through four parts:
1. The Constitutional Prologue
2. The Adversarial Crucible
3. Governance, Memory and Form
4. Threshold to Public Language

Alongside the volume, two satellite essays are already promoted in the public series:
- *Why Serious AI Governance Must Capture Decision Rationale*
- *Governance Does Not Eliminate Freedom*

This is part of a broader effort to treat AI governance not as decorative policy language, but as operational architecture.
