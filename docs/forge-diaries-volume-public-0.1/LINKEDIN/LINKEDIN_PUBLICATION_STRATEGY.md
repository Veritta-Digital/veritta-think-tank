# LinkedIn Publication Strategy

## Objective
Position **Forge Diaries — Public Volume 0.1** on LinkedIn as a premium, governed and intellectually serious public release on agentic AI governance.

## Recommended outward publishing model
### Layer 1 — Launch post
Use a short native LinkedIn post to announce the release and frame the volume's thesis.

### Layer 2 — LinkedIn article or newsletter edition
Use a longer-form surface to publish either:
- the Curator's Introduction;
- or a shortened adapted extract of the volume.

### Layer 3 — Satellite essays
Promote the already-portable essays:
- `07 — Why Serious AI Governance Must Capture Decision Rationale`
- `08 — Governance Does Not Eliminate Freedom`

These can circulate independently while pointing back to the volume.

## Recommended sequence
1. Publish launch post.
2. Publish article or newsletter edition built from the Curator's Introduction.
3. In the following days, publish satellite essay excerpts or short posts derived from Essays 07 and 08.

## Recommended positioning
Do not position the volume as a raw internal archive.
Position it as:
- a first governed public edition;
- a curated body of admissible writings;
- a contribution to serious discussion on agentic AI governance.

## Surface choice
### Personal profile
Best for founder-authored thought leadership and direct intellectual signature.

### LinkedIn Page
Best for institutional continuity, brand alignment and future serial publication.

### Newsletter option
Recommended if Verittà wants to build a recurring public series rather than a one-off announcement.
A newsletter can support recurring editions on V-COF, governance, continuity and the Forge Diaries line.

## Mobile note
LinkedIn posts can be created on mobile, but article creation is better treated as a desktop workflow.

## Portuguese strategy
Use Portuguese for primary audience reach in Brazil.
Use English either as:
- a parallel version;
- a later edition;
- or a separate newsletter/language lane.
