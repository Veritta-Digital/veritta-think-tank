# Editorial Method Note

This volume is assembled under a Verittà Archivarium Prime logic.

That means:
- drafts are transformed into chapters through governed assembly;
- public legibility matters as much as internal fidelity;
- raw source material is not treated as publication-ready by default;
- cross-document rhythm and hierarchy are part of the editorial method, not afterthoughts.

The volume therefore privileges:
- conceptual continuity;
- explicit sequencing;
- disciplined transitions;
- visible distinction between chapter, promoted essay, appendix and supporting note.
