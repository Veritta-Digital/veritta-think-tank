# Essays Promoted — Satellite Readings

This folder integrates the essays promoted outside the core chapter sequence into the unified editorial project of **Forge Diaries — Public Volume 0.1**.

## Why they are here
These essays remain autonomous pieces in `docs/series/`, but they belong to the same public architecture of the Forge Diaries program.

They are placed here as **satellite readings** rather than chapters because they perform a different function:
- they isolate one argument with sharper public legibility;
- they can circulate independently of the volume;
- they deepen parts of the volume without overloading its internal spine.

## Editorial rule
Satellite essays do not replace chapters.
They extend the public intelligibility of the volume through narrower, more portable forms.
