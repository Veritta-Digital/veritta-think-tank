# 08 — Governance Does Not Eliminate Freedom

**Canonical location:** `docs/series/08-governance-does-not-eliminate-freedom.md`

## Editorial role in the unified project
This essay is integrated here as a **satellite reading** of *Forge Diaries — Public Volume 0.1*.

Its function is to deepen **Part II — The Adversarial Crucible** by isolating one of the central tensions of the corpus: the difference between governed freedom and directionless arbitrariness.

## Public significance
This text gives the unified project a more portable and broadly discussable public thesis. It carries one of the strongest philosophical claims of the Forge Diaries program in a form that can circulate independently from the volume.
