# 07 — Why Serious AI Governance Must Capture Decision Rationale

**Canonical location:** `docs/series/07-why-serious-ai-governance-must-capture-decision-rationale.md`

## Editorial role in the unified project
This essay is integrated here as a **satellite reading** of *Forge Diaries — Public Volume 0.1*.

Its function is to deepen **Part III — Governance, Memory and Form** by isolating one of the volume's most transferable insights: governance is incomplete if it preserves execution while losing the reasons that made execution legitimate.

## Public significance
This is one of the clearest public-facing texts to emerge from the Forge Diaries program because it translates a private insight into a broadly legible claim about AI governance, institutional memory and operational epistemology.
